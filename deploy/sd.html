Help keep healthcare workers' families safe

This website is a volunteer project designed to connect property owners with healthcare workers that are looking for short-term housing during the COVID﻿-﻿19 outbreak in San Diego.

Specifically, we are looking for property owners that have available furnished suites. We are providing listings to healthcare workers on a daily basis.

Property Owners

House a healthcare worker

We request that properties be made available to healthcare workers at no cost or a reduced cost, pro-rated weekly to avoid putting a burden on health care workers that are having to pay for their regular mortgage or rent in addition to the cost of separate housing.

If you are unable to provide your property at no cost, we ask that you find a compromise between market rent, and the price that staff can reasonably afford. Our suggested guideline for cost in this scenario is $1000/month for a place that has two beds, inclusive of all utilities. In this example, it could house two healthcare workers at a cost of $500 each per month.

Properties must be furnished with a separate entry and wifi. Ideally propereties will also have a kitchen, on-site laundry, and be within a short commute of a major healthcare facility.

How does this work?

Once you submit your property with your pertinent property information, we will add it to the list provided to healthcare workers looking for housing. Workers will then reach out to you to form an agreement.

Frequently Asked Questions

I'm not a healthcare worker, can I apply for housing?

This service is primarily for healthcare workers who carry the highest risk of contracting COVID﻿-﻿19. However, we understand that all essential workers carry some risk and might be seeking temporary, low-cost housing. If you are concerned about exposing your household to COVID﻿-﻿19, we encourage you to apply.

Can I email you to say thank you?

Please refrain from any other communications to the email addresses provided to allow us to effectively administer requests and offers! If you like, you can share this link on social media and thank us there.

Can I contact you for media enquiries?

Yes! For media enquiries, please email tanto.g@outlook.com with "Media Inquiry" in the subject line.

Why wouldn't I just use Craigslist or Airbnb?

Unlike more general platforms, our service caters specifically to healthcare and other essential workers during the COVID﻿-﻿19 outbreak in Vancouver. We vet those who apply for housing to ensure their need is legitimate, so that low-cost housing offers are not taken advantage of.

Is this service available in other cities?

Yes! We have sites running in several US cities well as Canada, and are actively looking to expand.
